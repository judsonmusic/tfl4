Ideal Being: You will notice throughout the TFL assessment that we refer to well-being as Ideal Being. Webster defines well-being as the state of being comfortable, healthy, or happy. Ideal Being represents these elements but invites you to conceptualize your ideal state of being based on what is important to you. The TFL assessment will invite you to reflect on 15 dimensions of your life. Therefore, helping you conceptualize your Ideal Being.

This self-reflection tool allows you to take inventory of your life in a comprehensive manner. Reflecting on one area would be incomplete so by using our holistic approach you will be able to assess your life in 15 different dimensions. Through this experience we hope you gain valuable insight, a broader perspective of your total Ideal Being and that you are motivated to take action in the areas that are important to you. Being informed can empower you to become your greatest advocate.

Reflect over the past 3 months as you answer each question. There are no right or wrong answers so trust your gut and rate each dimension as authentically as possible. Knowing where you are is the first step in making mindful decisions on where you want to go.

You are just 20 minutes away from receiving a complete overview of your life in 15 different dimensions. Once you complete the TFL assessment you will have immediate access to your results and a personalized TFL Action Plan on each dimension which includes:

  • Insights and facts
  • TFL actions to consider
  • Self-guided exercises
  • Tools and resources

Before you leave the TFL site you will be able to print out your complete TFL Empowerment Guide which gives you information on all 15 dimensions. This will help you develop a game plan to take action.

Your privacy is a priority to us so please be assured that the information you share will be kept confidential.
