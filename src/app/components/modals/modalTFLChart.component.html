Your Train For Life (TFL) Chart and Action Plan

What is my TFL Chart?: It is an interactive chart that will provide you a comprehensive overview of how you ranked yourself in all of the dimensions of your life that you have unlocked. Each bar on the chart is color coded and represents the following:

  • How important the dimension is to you
  • How the dimension impacts your happiness
  • How the dimension impacts your work performance
  • How satisfied you are with the dimension
  • How actively you are working to improve and/or maintain the dimension
  • How motivated you are to improve and/or maintain the dimension

Your TFL Chart: Reflects how you responded to the above questions in your assessment and the bars populate your responses from a low of 20 to a high of 100. Your TFL Chart will only reflect data findings in the dimensions that you have unlocked.

How does my TFL chart work?: You can suppress and populate each bar on the chart by clicking and unclicking the words below the chart: Importance, Happiness, Performance, Satisfaction, Action, and Motivation. This interactive view will allow you to compare and contrast each dimension you have unlocked in all 6 areas.

Your Ideal Being Score: Reflects how you ranked your level of satisfaction in each dimension.

Stress Gauge: Your stress gauge reflects the level of stress you feel associated with each dimension. 20% reflects low satisfaction and 100% reflects high satisfaction.

Your TFL Action Plan: Once each dimension is unlocked you will have access to dimension specific information, tools and resources designed to help you develop a TFL Action Plan to improve your level of satisfaction.

Remember: By recognizing what impacts your total Ideal Being you can begin to take action in the dimensions which are most important to you. Have fun reviewing your charts and TFL Action Plans and remember self-awareness is the first step towards personal empowerment.

Important to note: Your TFL Chart and Acton Plan will only reflect information in the dimensions that you have unlocked. Enjoy unlocking each dimension.

Instructions: Click on the  Icon at the top of your TFL Dashboard page at any time for instructions on how to navigate the assessment site.
